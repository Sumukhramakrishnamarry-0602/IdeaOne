Founder Story

Built from two disciplines that aren't supposed to mix.

Sumukh Ramakrishna Marry is a first-year university student running a dual focus in Business Administration and Computer Science. That dual foundation — technical rigor plus business structure — is what powers IDEAONE's approach.

"I didn't want to start an agency that resells content advice. I wanted to build the backend first — the automation, the data layer, the actual engineering — and let the growth follow."

Before IDEAONE, that meant building combat sports analytics tools as a research project — structured data pulled from chaotic, unstructured environments. Same principle applies here: build the system that holds, then scale creators through it.
