FAQ

Questions,
answered honestly.

Can't find what you're looking for? Send us a message — we reply ourselves.

Do we have to use all three pillars — Media, Tech, and Analytics?

No. Most clients start with the Creator Launch System or a single pillar. The system is designed to work best together — Analytics shows you what Media and Tech are actually producing, which is usually why clients expand.

What platforms and tools do you work with?

On Media, we work across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and LinkedIn. For Tech, we build on clean object-oriented foundations (C++, Java) and commonly integrate Webflow, Notion, and automation tools like Zapier and Make. For Analytics, we typically build dashboards in Notion, Google Sheets, or Looker Studio depending on your existing stack.

Is IDEAONE a funded or established agency?

No — and we won't pretend otherwise. IDEAONE is pre-revenue and founder-led by two students. What we offer instead of a long track record is a structurally sound system, validated on 4+ creator builds, engineered on real CS and BBA foundations.

How is pricing structured?

We don't publish fixed retainers because we're not scaling existing infrastructure — we're building it with you. Every engagement starts with a free system audit, after which we propose a scope and a flat project fee or a lightweight monthly arrangement, depending on what you need.

What is the Creator Launch System?

It's our core offer — a packaged system for creators and rising students that includes premium hook templates, niche clarity frameworks, and backend automation architecture, so you can post and convert without rebuilding your process every week.

Do you work with brands outside the UAE?

Yes. We're based in DUBAI , UAE, but onboarding, builds, and reviews happen remotely via video call and shared dashboards, so location isn't a constraint.
